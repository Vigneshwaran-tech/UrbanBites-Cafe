Our Story & Passion

Founded in 2010, UrbanBites Café began as a small family kitchen with a big dream — to serve bold, honest food that brings people together.

Every dish tells a story. From our hand-picked ingredients to our carefully crafted recipes, we believe food should be an experience, not just a meal.

Meet Chef Marco

Head Chef & Co-founder

With over 20 years of culinary experience across Italy, France, and New York, Chef Marco brings a world-class touch to every UrbanBites creation.

His philosophy is simple: let the ingredients speak. Fresh, seasonal, and always cooked with heart.
