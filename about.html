Our Story

In 2010, a small family kitchen in downtown New York became something more. What started as weekend pop-up dinners for friends and neighbors grew into UrbanBites Café — a place where every dish tells a story.

Chef Marco and co-founder Elena believed that food should bring people together. They set out to create a space that felt like home, with flavors borrowed from Italy, France, and the streets of New York.

Today, UrbanBites serves over 200 guests daily, partners with local farms for the freshest ingredients, and has been recognized as one of the city's best café-restaurants. But at our core, we're still that small kitchen — cooking with love.

Meet Chef Marco

Head Chef & Co-founder

Born in Naples, trained in Paris, and seasoned on the streets of New York — Chef Marco brings a lifetime of culinary mastery to UrbanBites.

After spending 10 years in Michelin-star kitchens across Europe, Marco decided to do something different. He wanted to create a place where gourmet meets comfort, where fine dining techniques are used to make food everyone can enjoy.

His signature style? Bold flavors, simple presentations, and an obsession with quality that borders on madness. Ask anyone who's tasted his Smash Burger — they'll tell you it changed their life.

"I don't cook for awards. I cook so that when you take a bite, you close your eyes and smile. That's my award."
